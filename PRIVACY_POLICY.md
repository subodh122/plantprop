Privacy Policy for PlantProp

Introduction
PlantProp provides botanical information and plant identification. We respect your privacy and do not collect personal information for our own use.

Data Collection and Use
Camera We use your camera to take photos of plants for identification. These images are processed to provide results and are not stored on our servers.
Location We use your location to find trees in your area. Your coordinates are used only for the search and are not tracked or stored.
Local Storage Your saved plants and images are stored locally on your device. You can delete them at any time.

Third Party Services
We use Google Gemini AI and Wikipedia to provide plant details and images. These services have their own privacy policies.

Data Sharing
We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Your Consent
By using the app you agree to the use of camera and location permissions for these features.
